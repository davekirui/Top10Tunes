About!

This app is an online version of my book of music lists, DJ Pascoe's Top 10 Tunes. It is

aimed at DJs, and other people who love and are interested in music, and it is designed

to give you easy access to ideas about particular tracks to play or talk about across a

wide range of musical genres.

Each top ten is based on a combination of the "must play lists I always ask my clients to

provide, the most popular requests I have received while djing over the years, my own

personal likes, and the views of some of my music loving friends and fellow DJs. The

tracks I selected for my top tens are, in general, more "populist" than "purist". I'm not

saying they are the "best" in any of the given categories, just that they are the ones that

most people seem to like.....in my experience.

Almost all of the tracks listed here have a live link to their Youtube video, so you can

check them out for yourself, and new lists will be added on a regular basis.

The main purpose of these lists is to help to DJs to find those magic combinations of

music that will make a party swing, no matter the circumstances or the makeup of the

crowd on the dance floor. I also hope they will generate lots of healthy discussion and

debate about music. You are ENCOURAGED to become a part of this conversation by

checking out the Top Ten Tunes website at www.top10tunes.co.uk and sharingu989= YOUR

top 10 lists, or your view on where you think a particular song should be rated. Help me

build a MASSIVE database which will be the go-to reference point for DJs and party

people from all over the world who are looking to engage with new kinds of music.
